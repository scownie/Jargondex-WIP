In collaboration with Computing, Intel is sponsoring a competition for UK IT professionals to win a Christmas weekend trip to Prague. Launched last night at the UK IT awards, in the run up to the festive season the competition is set to find Santa’s next CIO. The ideal candidate will be a truly forward-thinking visionary who can add the magic of technology to the magic of Christmas, in line with the corporate strategy and the core values of the Christmas season.  Entrants can apply to be Santa’s next CIO here.
