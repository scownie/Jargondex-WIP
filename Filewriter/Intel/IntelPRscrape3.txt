Today, Nokia Networks and Intel announced the opening a new innovation center located at an Intel facility in Bath, UK. The first of its kind lab will accelerate development of new mobile broadband applications and services enabled by intelligent base-stations. By equipping developers with Nokia Networks’ Liquid Applications platform powered by Intel® based intelligent data processing, distributed Cloud compute capabilities will be located closer to mobile phone users enabling a new breed of services, including proximity and context-based features and other localized services.
