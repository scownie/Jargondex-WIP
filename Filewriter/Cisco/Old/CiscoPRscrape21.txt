PRESS RELEASE Cisco Schedules Conference Call for Q1 Fiscal Year 2015 Financial Results SAN JOSE, Calif, --- October 29, 2014  --- Cisco has scheduled a conference call for Wednesday, November 12, 2014, at 1:30 PM (PT) to announce its first quarter fiscal year 2015 financial results for the period ending Saturday, October 25, 2014. Financial results will be released over Marketwired via US National, European Technology Trade Media and Western Europe distribution circuits, after the close of the market on Wednesday, November 12, 2014. Cisco's quarterly earnings press release will be posted at www.cisco.com under the "Newsroom" section.
